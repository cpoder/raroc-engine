OpenRAROC — Bank data from public Pillar 3 CR6 regulatory filings

OpenRAROC is a free, open-source RAROC (Risk-Adjusted Return on Capital) calculator for corporate treasurers and CFOs. Compare credit facility pricing across 59 global banks using real Basel III Pillar 3 regulatory filings. Optimize your bank allocation to minimize borrowing costs. Generate professional credit profile reports for board presentations.
